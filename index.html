How We Work

Waste management

Garbage is one of the biggest problems faced by developing countries. The existing methods of waste disposal such as open dumping, incineration, landfills, have all proven to have great disadvantages. Subcritical water waste treatment could be the answer to the waste disposal crisis of the world. Our equipment can treat all kinds of wastes that including organic, plastic, and medical wastes. The byproduct can be used either as a fertilizer or a fuel, depending on the waste that is processed.

EV Conversion

To overcome the issue of air pollution we are working on converting the traditional IC engine rickshaws to Electric powered motor rickshaws. These can minimize the release of carbon dioxide into the atmosphere. Since there is a shortage of charging stations, we are developing movable charging stations. These stations are IoT enabled networks that will help the auto owners to exchange the drained battery for a fully charged battery pack.

Inactivating COVID-19

We are developing an air purifier to improve the air quality by inactivating the harmful viruses and reducing air pollutants. We strive to bring back the time and life of the people as it was before the outbreak of COVID-19. Therefore, our research involves the study of an air purifier with an in-built ultraviolet lamp and nanoparticle coated filter that can be used in the presence of humans, ensuring their safety.

Smart City and Smart Farm

By using emerging technologies like the IoT, we aim to solve problems faced by Indian farmers such as agricultural management, climate change (droughts, floods, etc.), shortage of laborers, literacy of crop cultivation, and government schemes. IoT devices and Data analysis are used for "saving power”, "optimum water usage", “water monitoring system”, “weather data utilization”, “loan monitoring system”, and many more.

Japanese Language Training

At MOSAIQUE, we provide Japanese language education to students who dream to kick start their career in Japan. Since, one cannot go to Japan and work by just speaking the language, we truly focus on technical skills enhancement along with language and life skill development.
